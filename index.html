Welcome to my webpage

I defended my PhD, directed by Luis Almeida and Jean-Michel Coron on 05th september 2025, which was entitled "Feedback stabilisation of a sterile insect control system: Applications to mosquito-borne disease control."

SIT is currently used to reduce the populations of insect vectors (which transmit diseases) and pests (which damage agricultural crops).
