Welcome to Kagbobidi

Since 2022, I have been a PhD student in the LJLL laboratory at Sorbonne University under the supervision of Luis Almeida and Jean-Michel Coron.

My research focuses on feedback stabilisation of dynamical systems modeling insect population control using the Sterile Insect Technique (SIT).

SIT is currently used to reduce the populations of insect vectors (which transmit diseases) and pests (which damage agricultural crops).
